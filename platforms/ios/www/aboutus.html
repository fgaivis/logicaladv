About Us

What We Do

At Logical Advantage, we offer a suite of customized services to help your organization realize IT success. With our proven methodologies, keen understanding of business operations and an unyielding commitment to quality, we bridge the gap between resources and results.

Our Mission

Our mission is to partner with our clients to provide strategies and solutions that maximize the ROI of Enterprise Asset Intelligence and Human Capital Development.

Industry Involvement

At Logical Advantage, we believe the health of our organization is a reflection of the health of our community. With that in mind, we proudly support these organizations who foster development of the IT and business community.

Giving

Each year, Logical Advantage selects multiple organizations that are important to our staff. These include organizations that are supported by our staff or have supported our staff directly. Rather than sending gifts to our clients during the holidays, we make donations to these organizations on their behalf. Since 2003, in addition to non-monetary support, such as time and materials, Logical Advantage has donated over $10,000.
